## Who We're Targeting

**Primary segment:** CTOs and VPs of Engineering at scaling SaaS/tech companies (100–1000 employees, Series A–C) where engineering growth has outpaced process maturity.

**Secondary segment:** CPOs, COOs, and Digital Transformation leads at traditional companies (500–5000 employees) going through digital transformation and needing agile ways of working.

## Contact Profile

**Primary target titles (in priority order):**
1. CTO / Chief Technology Officer
2. VP Engineering / VP of Engineering
3. Head of Engineering / Director of Engineering
4. CPO (if no CTO found at company)

**Secondary target titles:**
- COO (at transformation-led companies)
- CDO / Chief Digital Officer
- Head of Digital Transformation

**One contact per company only** — the most senior tech/product decision maker.

---

## What Good Looks Like

A strong-fit lead has 3+ of the following:

- [ ] Title is CTO, VP Eng, or Head of Eng at a 100–1000 person company
- [ ] Company raised Series B or C in the last 18 months
- [ ] Company is actively hiring Scrum Masters, Engineering Managers, or Delivery Managers
- [ ] Prospect has posted on LinkedIn about delivery, agile, sprints, or team scaling in the last 30 days
- [ ] Company has had a leadership change (new CTO, new CPO) in the last 12 months
- [ ] Company announced a digital transformation programme or acquisition
- [ ] Engineering team grew > 30% in the last 12 months

---

## What Bad Looks Like (Disqualifiers)

**Hard disqualifiers — never contact:**
- Company is itself an agile/scrum consultancy
- Company has > 10 Scrum Masters on staff (mature practice)
- Company < 100 employees (too early)
- Company > 10,000 employees (too large, have internal programmes)
- Lead previously unsubscribed or gave a hard no

**Soft disqualifiers — flag for review:**
- Company shows no tech/engineering hiring signals
- Prospect title is below Director level
- Company is in a non-digital industry with no transformation signal
- Company is in active financial distress (layoffs > 20% in last 6 months)
