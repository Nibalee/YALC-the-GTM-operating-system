# Value Propositions — Alacient

## Core Positioning

**Alacient embeds agile practitioners directly inside engineering and product teams at scaling companies — not training, not frameworks, but doing it alongside them until it sticks.**

---

## By Audience

### CTO / VP Engineering at Series B–C Company

**Their world:** Engineering grew from 10 to 100 people. What worked at 10 doesn't work at 100. Sprints are chaotic. Stakeholders are frustrated. Roadmap is slipping. They're drowning in people management instead of building.

**Their goal:** Predictable delivery. Calm engineering org. Stakeholders who trust the timeline.

**Our value:** We've fixed this specific problem at companies at exactly your stage. We come in, work inside your team, and build the muscle memory for how to run sprints, manage cross-team dependencies, and communicate delivery with confidence. Most clients feel the shift in the first sprint.

**The number:** 40% improvement in sprint predictability within 60 days, average.

**How to use this in copy:**
> "We helped a Series B SaaS team go from missing 40% of sprints to 90%+ predictability in 60 days. Might be worth 20 minutes to see if the same approach fits [Company]."

---

### CPO / Head of Product at Transformation Company

**Their world:** Tech team is waterfall. Product team wants to ship every 2 weeks. The gap between how product thinks and how engineering works is creating friction, missed dates, and frustrated customers.

**Their goal:** Product and engineering speaking the same language and shipping together.

**Our value:** We bridge product and engineering through shared agile practice. We don't just coach the engineers — we align the whole delivery system so product decisions and engineering execution move together.

**How to use this in copy:**
> "Most product-engineering misalignment isn't a people problem — it's a process problem. We fix the process."

---

### COO / CDO at Digital Transformation Programme

**Their world:** Board has mandated digital transformation. They've hired a digital team. But the rest of the organisation still works the old way. The transformation is stalling because the process isn't there.

**Their goal:** Digital delivery that actually delivers. Agile ways of working that spread beyond the digital team.

**Our value:** We've run agile adoption at enterprise scale across multiple functions. We know how to make it stick in organisations that weren't built for it.

## Differentiation vs Alternatives

### vs. Internal Hiring

"Hiring a Scrum Master takes 6-12 months to find and ramp. We have impact in week one."

### vs. Big 4 / Large Consultancies

"We're hands-on practitioners, not advisors. No frameworks dropped from a slide deck. We work in your team."

### vs. SAFe Certification / Training

"We're not a framework. We adapt to your context. If SAFe fits, we can implement it. If it doesn't, we build something better."

### vs. "Doing it ourselves"

"Most teams try this themselves first. We're usually called in when they've realised it's harder than it looks. We accelerate what you're trying to do."

---

## What We Are NOT

Never position Alacient as:
- A staffing agency ("we'll send you a Scrum Master")
- A training company ("we'll run a 2-day agile workshop")
- A framework vendor ("you need to implement SAFe")
- A generic management consultant ("transformation programme")

We are: **practitioners who embed inside your team and build the capability from the inside out.**
